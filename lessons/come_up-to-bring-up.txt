===================================
	TEMA: TO COME UP - TO BRING UP
===================================
TO COME UP
	Es un Verbo frasal, o un verbo compuesto que significa (dependiendo de su contexto)
	-> surgir
		Ej. If something COMES UP, let me know - Si algo surge, me avisas.

	Tambien significa: Acércate.
		EJ. Come up here - Acércate aquí
		Ej. Walk up to him - Acércate a el caminando
		Ej. Go up to him - Acércate a el
		Ej. Adrive up there - Acércate allí en coche
		Ej. He swem up to me


TO BRING UP
	-> significa, Sacar un tema, traer a colación.
		EJ. If you wanna talk about that in the meeting tomorrow, then BRING IT UP

		EJ. If it doesn't come up in the meeting, then bring it up.

		EJ. If it doesn't come up, bring it up so we can talk about it.

	Tambien tiene este uso:
		Ej. He was brought up in the USA - El se crió en ..
